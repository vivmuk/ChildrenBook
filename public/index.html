Frequently asked questions

How does Story Spark work?

Provide a prompt, select preferences, and our Venice.ai pipeline generates the narrative, characters, and illustrations in minutes.

Is my child's information safe?

Absolutely. We only use the details you provide to build the story and never store sensitive information.

How can I contact support?

Send us an email at support@storyspark.app and our team will be happy to help.

Can I customise the art style?

Yes! Pick from dozens of curated art styles and Venice.ai will apply your choice across every page consistently.
